[Gunship](https://www.gunshipmusic.com/) is a retro synthwave artist out of the UK.

[Revel in Your Time](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uYRZV8dV10w), [Tech Noir](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-nC5TBv3sfU), [Fly for Your Life](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jv1ZN8c4_Gs) and [The Mountain](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-HYRTJr8EyA) are all quality songs by Gunship. Check out those amazing music videos!

Also take a look at other retro synthwave artists such as [Trevor Something](https://trevorsomething.bandcamp.com/), [Droid Bishop](https://droidbishop.bandcamp.com/), [FM-84](https://fm84.bandcamp.com/) and [Daniel Deluxe](https://danieldeluxe.bandcamp.com/).
